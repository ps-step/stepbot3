Features

  • Question Database: STEP 2 and 3 from 2008 to 2024. More questions will be added if I can be bothered, but this is unlikely.
  • Practice Mode: Filter questions by paper, type, and topic. You can track your progress in the sidebar, which filters based on selected topics.
  • Mock Mode: Generate timed mock papers consisting of 12 questions you have not done. Grade boundaries interpolated from years.
  • Revision Mock: Re-test yourself on questions you've already done! Hidden marks until you click finish.
  • Progress Tracking: Marks questions as complete, logs your scores, and saves your notes automatically to your browser.
  • Mark Schemes: View PDF mark schemes within the app.

Note: Unless you are signed in, all data is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser cache will remove your progress. Signing in fixes this!

Edexcel A Level Maths

  • Past papers: PMT, Crash Maths, Madas Maths, Edexcel Mock Sets, probably more.

  • Textbooks: Edexcel Textbooks

TMUA, MAT, Senior Maths Challenge, and Senior Kangaroo

  • R2Drew2 goes through all the past papers and has teaching videos too.
  • TMUA past papers
  • TMUA 'fanmade' past papers:
    • The papers by Tyler Tutoring, which are four of each, are probably slightly easier than the real paper.
    • The papers by Yotta are diabolical. They are meant to be harder than a real paper.
    • The papers by James are quite a bit easier than the real TMUA - I don’t think they’re worth spending much time on.
    • The papers by Asher Falcon are also quite a bit easier.
    • The papers by Beyond Horizon are probably a touch harder than the real paper.
    • I haven’t done the papers by Zetta or Oxbridge Applications.
    • There is also a MEGA folder with two papers by STEPMaths which I couldn’t be bothered to add to Google Drive.
    • There is also a 2024 Chinese TMUA Paper which consists of questions recalled by students after the 2024 exam.
  • MAT past papers
  • Senior Maths Challenge past papers and Senior Kangaroo past papers are interchangable probably, similar sort of exam.
  • MAT livestreams
  • Siklos book intends to prepare students for university entrance exams including TMUA. I don’t think it’s that exceptional, especially for TMUA, though.
  • This STEP, MAT, TMUA textbook… exists, but I don't think anyone really uses this.

BMO

  • Past papers
  • There are also decades of BMO1 and BMO2 questions with video solutions on the UKMT website.

Oxbridge Interviews

  • This Google Drive has all the interview questions I could find. The following were the most useful to me:
    • The Oxford interview questionnaires, handwritten notes compiled by people immediately after their interviews, and sheets with headings like ‘Interview 1’ and ‘Interview 2’ are, as the name suggests, peoples’ actual interview questions.
    • The Jesus College Specimen Admissions Test is meant to be more of a pre-interview admissions test, which is what some colleges do rather than just chucking you straight into an interview, but this is essentially interview questions so is also useful.
    • I can’t remember who they’re by, but the sheets with stuff which looks like essentially STEP questions with summations and integrals were very good, and much more like interview questions than they look.
    • The following problem sheets were all very good in terms of difficulty:
      • ‘Problem Sheet - Oxbridge Maths Interview Questions’ by Isaac Tseng
      • ‘Interview Questions’ by Flour
      • ‘Oxbridge Interview Questions for Mathematics and Sciences’
      • ‘Maths Interview Questions’ from The Student Room
      • ‘Maths Interview Questions’ by Vishal Gupta
      • ‘TBO Problem Solving Booklet’ by TBO - the goat!
      • ‘Interview Problem Set 1’ by Alfie Thompson
      • ‘Maths at University Interview Questions’ (but only the first section)
      • ‘Curve Sketching Questions’ by Waleed
      • ‘Maths Interview Questions’ by JM Stone
  • This Google Document has some more interview questions.
  • This Google Document by Opsimath on a Chinese forum is very nice, you can find solutions on his profile.
