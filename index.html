Features

  • Question Database: STEP 2 and 3 from 2008 to 2024. More questions will be added if I can be bothered.
  • Practice Mode: Filter questions by paper, type, and topic.
  • Mock Mode: Generate timed mock papers consisting of 12 questions you have not done. Grade boundaries interpolated from years.
  • Progress Tracking: Marks questions as complete, logs your scores, and saves your notes automatically to your browser.
  • Mark Schemes: View PDF mark schemes within the app.

Note: All data is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser cache will remove your progress.

Examination Strategy

1. Selection: Read through the first few questions of each section. Choose the ones that look most approachable first.

2. Presentation: Write clear, logical steps. Examiners award marks for method even if the final answer is wrong.

3. Time Management: Do not spend more than 45 minutes on a question unless you are certain you can finish it.
